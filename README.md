# 🌊 AI-Driven Unified Data Platform for Oceanographic & Biodiversity Insights

An AI-powered platform that integrates multiple environmental datasets to analyze ocean conditions, detect ecological anomalies, and identify marine risk zones.

The system processes multi-source environmental data such as rainfall, sea surface temperature, NDVI vegetation signals, and river discharge to estimate coastal ecological stress using a custom risk metric called **RM-NPI (River Mouth Nutrient Pressure Index)**.

The platform combines **machine learning, environmental analytics, and geospatial visualization** to provide interpretable insights into marine ecosystem vulnerability.

---

# 🚀 Key Features

- Multi-source environmental dataset ingestion
- Automated data preprocessing and spatial alignment
- AI representation learning using an autoencoder architecture
- RM-NPI risk estimation for coastal nutrient pressure
- Environmental anomaly detection
- Ocean ecological risk zone mapping
- Interactive visualization dashboards
- Explainable environmental risk analysis

| S | Seasonal intensity factor |
| D | Distance decay from river mouth |

Higher RM-NPI values indicate **greater nutrient pressure entering marine ecosystems**, which may lead to:

- Algal blooms
- Oxygen depletion
- Marine biodiversity stress
- Coastal pollution events
